## Schriftliche Berechnung der Gleichung der Normalen
$f(x) = ...$
$f'(x) = ...$
$f'(x) = ... = m$
$m = -\frac1{m_t}$
$f(x_P) = ... = y_P$
$y = mx + n$
$y_P = m_1*x_P + n$
$n = ...$
Normale: $y = m_1x + n$
