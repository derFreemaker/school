### a)
$f(x) = (3x + 1) *e^x$
$f'(x) = 3 *e^x + (3x + 1) *e^x = e^x(3 +3x +1)$
$0 = e^x(3 +3x +1)$
$x_1 = \frac43$

### b)
$f(x) = 4x^2 *e^x$
$f'(x) = 8x *e^x + 4x^2 *e^x = e^x(8x +4x^2)$
$0 = e^x(8x +4x^2)$ 
$x_1 = -2$
